Cinderella

About the Movie

"Cinderella" (2015) is a romantic fantasy film directed by Kenneth Branagh. The film follows Ella, a kind and gentle young woman, who is mistreated by her stepmother and stepsisters but never loses hope. With the magical help of her Fairy Godmother, she attends the royal ball and wins the heart of Prince Kit.

Cast

  • Lily James as Cinderella
  • Richard Madden as Prince Kit
  • Cate Blanchett as Lady Tremaine
  • Helena Bonham Carter as Fairy Godmother
  • Stellan Skarsgård as Grand Duke

Release Date

March 13, 2015
